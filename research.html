Focusing on the Digital Transformation of firms and society, as well as on the responsible design and use of AI-based services, Christian Matt’s research projects often pursue interdisciplinary approaches to discover the socio-technical effects that digital technologies have on different stakeholders. His {% include links/external.html url="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=HSsnJNgAAAAJ&hl=en&oi=sra" name="research"%} has been published in international top-notch academic and practice-oriented journals (e.g., MIS Quarterly, Journal of Management Information Systems, Journal of Information Technology, MIS Quarterly Executive). Christian Matt currently serves as a Senior Editor of the journals Electronic Markets and Internet Research.
